ABOUT THE BUSINESS

ShedHeadMinis was founded by Matthew Faulkenbury in October of 2014. Over the past 11 years the company has sent minis all over the world for a variety of different board games such as Dungeons & Dragons, Warhammer 40k, Age of Sigmar, and many, many more. Our goal is to bring your models to life on the tabletop by utilizing a plethora of techniques, tools, and materials acquired over the course of over twenty years in the business.
