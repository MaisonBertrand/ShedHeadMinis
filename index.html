ABOUT THE ARTIST

Matthew Faulkenbury

I have been painting miniatures for just over twenty years, if you count me being 7, and began taking commissions when I was in high-school. I have worked closely with clients for the better part of 2 decades even before ShedHeadMinis to create many vibrant works of art for the tabletop. From custom conversions of a single hero character to entire Armies with gaming boards to match, I am your one stop shop for anything you need customized or painted.
